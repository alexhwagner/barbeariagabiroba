Design Guidelines for BARBEARIA GABIROBA Landing Page
Design Approach
Premium Barber Shop Aesthetic - Modern, masculine, elegant design drawing inspiration from high-end grooming establishments with a distinctive green-neon signature theme.

Core Design Elements
Color Palette
Primary Background: Deep black to dark graphite (#0a0a0a to #1a1a1a)
Neon Accent: Electric green (#00ff41, #39ff14) with glowing effects
Text: White to light gray for contrast
Cards/Surfaces: Dark gray with subtle transparency
Typography
Headings: Bold, modern sans-serif with strong presence (Bebas Neue, Oswald, or Barlow Condensed style)
Body Text: Clean, professional sans-serif (Roboto, Inter, or Poppins)
Neon Title Treatment: "BARBEARIA GABIROBA" with green glow shadow effects
Hierarchy: Large hero titles (60-80px desktop), medium section headers (36-48px), readable body text (16-18px)
Layout System
Spacing: Tailwind units of 4, 8, 12, 16, 20, 24 (e.g., p-8, gap-12, py-20)
Section Padding: py-16 to py-24 for consistent vertical rhythm
Container: max-w-7xl for main content areas
Grid Systems: 3-column for services (stacks to 1 on mobile), masonry-style for gallery
Component Library
Navigation

Sticky header, initially transparent with blur effect, transitions to solid dark (#0a0a0a) on scroll
Logo on left, navigation links center/right, green neon underline on active/hover
Mobile: Hamburger menu with slide-in panel
Hero Section

Layout: Fullscreen (100vh) with centered content
Background: Dark barber shop image (modern interior with leather chairs, mirrors, warm ambient lighting) with dark overlay (rgba(0,0,0,0.7))
Title: Large "BARBEARIA GABIROBA" with animated green neon glow pulsing effect
Subtitle: "Estilo, precisão e o corte que combina com você" in lighter weight
CTAs: Two buttons side-by-side - "Agendar Agora" (primary green-neon fill) and "Falar no WhatsApp" (green-neon outline) with blur backdrop
Decorative Elements: Subtle animated neon particles or glow lines floating in background
About Section

2-column layout: Text left, 3 animated icon cards right
Icons representing: Experience (scissors), Style (comb), Quality (star/badge)
Cards with dark background, green-neon borders that glow on hover
Fade-in animations on scroll
Services Cards

3-column grid (2-column tablet, 1-column mobile)
Each card: Icon at top, service name, brief description
Services: Corte Masculino, Barba Completa, Navalhado Premium, Degradê, Sobrancelha, Pacotes Especiais
Dark card backgrounds with soft shadows
Green-neon outline that intensifies on hover
Icons: Barber-themed (scissors, razor, beard, comb) in green-neon style
Slide-up animation on scroll
Gallery

4-column masonry grid (3-col tablet, 2-col mobile)
Modern men's haircut placeholder images (fades, pompadours, undercuts, beard styles)
Zoom effect on hover with green-neon border glow
Fade-in stagger animation on scroll
Location Section

2-column split: Google Maps iframe left (60%), address info right (40%)
Full address with pin icon
"Abrir no Google Maps" button in green-neon style
Dark background with subtle green accent
Contact Section

2-column: Contact form left, quick contact info right
Form fields: Name, Phone, Message with green-neon focus states
Quick contact: WhatsApp (clickable), Phone, Business Hours
Green-neon submit button
Footer

3-column layout: Business info, Quick links, Social media placeholders
Dark background with green-neon separator line at top
Dynamic copyright year (JavaScript)
Minimal, elegant spacing
Floating Elements

WhatsApp button: Fixed bottom-right, circular, green-neon (#25D366) with pulse animation
Animations & Interactions
Scroll Animations: Use AOS.js library
Fade-in for text blocks
Slide-up for cards
Stagger for gallery items
Hover States: Neon glow intensification (box-shadow expansion)
Button Interactions: Green-neon glow on hover, slight scale transform
Header Transition: Smooth 0.3s ease for transparency to solid
Neon Effects: CSS text-shadow and box-shadow with green layers creating glow
Images
Hero Background: Modern barber shop interior - leather chairs, vintage mirrors, warm Edison bulbs, dark wood accents, professional grooming tools visible, atmospheric lighting

Gallery Images (8-12 placeholders):

Clean fade haircuts
Sharp beard trims
Modern pompadour styles
Undercut designs
Before/after transformations
Barber action shots (trimming, styling)
Accessibility & Responsiveness
Maintain consistent green-neon contrast ratios against dark backgrounds
Mobile: Stack all multi-column layouts to single column
Touch-friendly button sizes (min 44px height)
Smooth scroll behavior across all devices
Form inputs with clear focus states
Key Visual Principles
Premium Feel: Polished, professional, never cheap
Masculine Energy: Bold typography, strong contrasts, decisive layouts
Neon Signature: Green glow effects used strategically, not overwhelming
Modern Sophistication: Clean lines, ample spacing, refined details
